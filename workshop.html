Project 1:
Fusion 360

In Fusion 360, I created 3D design of a NameTag.

Using Commands: Fillet to smoothen out the sharp egdes and making it rounder,
Creating a Sketch to be able to create a 2D design first,
Extrude to transform the 2D sketch into a 3D design,
Text to be able to type my name into the surface of the design and extruding
it afterwards to have my name appearing to Pop Out.


I also created a 3D design of a Queen Chesspiece.

Project 3:
3D Printing

The 3D printer used is the Ultimaker.
In order to 3D print our Fusion 360 models,
it is required to convert the .F3D file into a .stl file first

After it is done, Ultimaker Cura Application is required to be able for
modifications of the 3D print specifications

For Example:

Nozzle Size: The thinner it is, the more smooth the 3D print will be.

Project 4:
T-Shirt Design and Printing

I created a design of my shirt using Canva as it was very easy to use and does not require
download.
Exporting it in SVG format whuch makes use of Vectors makes the image quality
much more sharper than PNG format.

I've always been fascinated with Space and Pirates
so I decided to blend both elements into one with the Pirate logo and
Spaceship
to make it look super SICK.

At first, I was really nervous about the process of heating my
shirt as it is dangerous but
the Seniors were really friendly making the process less intense.

First step was to iron the shirt for 5 seconds to
make sure there are no wrinkles.

After that I had to paste the printed designs that was
printed using a special
kind of INK paper at the
shirt.

With that comes the final heating process which takes 45 seconds.
